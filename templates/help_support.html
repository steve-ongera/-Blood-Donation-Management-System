Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new donor to the system?
Go to the Donors section and click "Add Donor" button. Fill in all required information and save.
How can I check blood inventory levels?
Navigate to the Blood Stock section to view current inventory levels by blood group.
What should I do if I forgot my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
